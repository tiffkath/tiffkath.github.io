Beetle Innards is an Interactive Color Wheel made from Acrylic, ink, iridescent nail polish. The organic shell shape was made from vacuum-forming acrylic over a cabbage.

The Shirts were all Hand-Bleached & Screen Printed by my own hand with screens I built with the help of Hugo Hernandez. 45% off profits went to 714MutualAid (A food distribution organization in OC) with a $400 total.
